# Synthesis dataset overview
Synthesis datasets are usually organised in groups of datasets rather than individual datasets. We provide the following overview from January 2023 of current Synthesis datasets in BExIS and how they are related.
Please refer to our [manual](https://github.com/biodiversity-exploratories-synthesis/Synthesis_dataset_manual/blob/main/Synthesis%20datasets%20%20How%20to%20use.md) to know how to use these datasets.

The datasets belong to the following groups of indicators : 

- Diversity Grassland
- Diversity Forest
- Ecosystem Functions Grassland
- Plant Traits
- Landscape land-use and Environmental Covariates
